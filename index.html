Objective Of DPMS

The Digital Project Management System (DPMS) is dedicated to ensuring that every rural household has access to an adequate and reliable drinking water supply, meeting prescribed quality standards on a regular and long-term basis. Our mission is to enhance the living standards of rural communities by delivering sustainable and affordable water services.
